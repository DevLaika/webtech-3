Who we are

Burgerbar is a (delivery & takeaway) restaurant with delicious Burgers. At Burgerbar we use fresh minced meat every day for our burgers.

Where we are

We are located in The Netherlands, Utrecht, Schoutenstraat 15 3512GA.

Our ingredients

Our products are extra distinctive and tasteful thanks to our attention to the environment and sustainability. All our vegetables are organic and our meat comes from free-ranging animals. The fries are freshly cut every day in the morning. In addition, our milkshakes are made from freshly turned organic ice cream.
