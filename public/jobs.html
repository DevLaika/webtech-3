Working at Burgerbar

Cashier

As a Cashier you are the face of the company. You take the order of the customers with a smile. You are in responsible for the processing and receiving of payments.

Burger Flipper

As a Burger Flipper you are part of our cooking team. Your main task is to grill and flip the burgers. But ofcourse you do plenty of other stuff as well. For example frying our delicious french fries.

Cleaner

Our burgers have to taste delicious every day. Cleaning our kitchen is very important to maintain the good taste of our product. As a Cleaner you make sure that and the end of the day everything is as new!

Office Employeer

Besides jobs at our Burgerbars, there are plenty of office jobs in our Headquater located in Amsterdam. Come take a look behind the screen.
